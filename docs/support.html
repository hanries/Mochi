Logging food

Tap Feed Mochi · log a meal to scan a photo of your food, or use More ways to log to search the food database or add a food manually. You can scan with the camera or pick an existing photo from your library. After a scan, you can correct any item — its name (which re-searches for accurate nutrition), its portion, or remove it — before saving.

Editing or deleting an entry

Tap any logged food to edit its details or delete it. Custom foods you create are saved so you can quickly log them again.

Weight & photo journal

In the Log tab you can record your weight and keep a photo journal. You can add more than one photo per day, and each entry can hold several pictures.

Mochi Premium

Premium unlocks unlimited AI food scans. Everything else stays free.

  • Restore a purchase: open the Premium screen and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Manage or cancel: subscriptions are billed by Apple — manage them in iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. Cancelling there stops future renewals; you keep Premium until the period ends.
  • Free trial: if your plan includes a trial, you're only charged when it ends, and you can cancel anytime before then.

Reminders

Mochi can send one gentle check-in a day if you allow notifications. Turn them on or off any time in iPhone Settings → Notifications → Mochi.

Your data

Your logs, weight, and photos are stored on your device. Deleting the app removes them. See our Privacy Policy for details.
